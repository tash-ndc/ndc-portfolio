_About

My name is Natasha, but I usually go by Tash. I currently used to work in retail, but decided to move on to web design. I became interested in web design when I was a tween, and self-taught myself (badly) some HTML and CSS. After completing a Foundation Degree in Graphics, I found I was far more interested in coding the sites rather than designing the imaging that makes them up, so that's what I've gone back to!

I'm based in Milton Keynes, England, right around the corner from the Open University where I'm studying part-time towards a BSc (Honours) in Computing and IT. I'm also going through a programming course, and hope to use this to get a job in the industry.

I'm always interested in a challenge. Reach out to me here!
